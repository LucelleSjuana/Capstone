our Chiropractic services

Spinal Adjustment

Also known as spinal manipulation, this is the most well-known chiropractic technique. It involves applying controlled, sudden force to specific joints to realign the spine and restore proper function to the nervous system.

Soft Tissue Therapies

Chiropractors use various soft tissue techniques to address muscle tension, scar tissue, and trigger points. These therapies can include massage, myofascial release, and instrument-assisted soft tissue mobilization.

Joint Mobilization

It is a gentle, hands-on approach that involves the passive movement of a joint within its normal range of motion. Chiropractors use joint mobilization to treat various musculoskeletal conditions, alleviate pain, reduce stiffness, and enhance overall joint health.

Corrective Exercises

Chiropractors often prescribe specific exercises to improve posture, strengthen weak muscles, and promote better spinal alignment.

Postural Correction

Poor posture can lead to a range of health issues. We provide postural assessments and corrective exercises to promote better alignment and prevent future problems.

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Whether you're a professional athlete or a weekend warrior, our sports injury rehabilitation services are designed to help you recover from injuries and enhance athletic performance.
